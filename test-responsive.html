Testing Instructions:

  1. Resize your browser window to test different screen sizes
  2. Use Chrome DevTools (F12) and toggle device toolbar (Ctrl+Shift+M)
  3. Test common device sizes:
    • iPhone SE: 375x667
    • iPhone 12 Pro: 390x844
    • iPad: 768x1024
    • iPad Pro: 1024x1366
  4. Check that the hamburger menu appears on mobile/tablet
  5. Verify text is readable on all screen sizes
  6. Ensure buttons and links are easily tappable on mobile
